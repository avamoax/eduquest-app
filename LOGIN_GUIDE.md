# 🚀 EduQuest Login Guide

## ✅ **Login System Ready!**

Your EduQuest app features a complete authentication system with multiple login options.

## 🎯 **How to Login:**

### **Option 1: Google Sign-In**
1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/`
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Wait for authentication
4. Your profile will appear with a continue button

### **Option 2: Email/Password**
1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/`
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password (6+ characters)
4. Click **"Login"** or **"Create Account"**
5. Your profile will appear with a continue button

## 🎉 **After Successful Login:**

### **Profile Display:**
- ✅ Profile picture or avatar

### **Complete User Journey:**
1. **Login** → Profile appears
2. **Click Continue** → Age selection page
